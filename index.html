    What is Trump Coin?

    We unite under the shared admiration of Trump

    The TrumpCoin ecosystem was established to provide an engaging experience for like-minded individuals who (primarily) share Donald Trumps values, are excited about the future of America and have an interest in the XRP Ledger. More than 80% of $TRUMP is in circulation (via airdrops with only 1% sold via pre-sale) and 15% of supply has been reserved for farming rewards.

    TrumpCoin is operated under a Decentralised Autonomous Organisation (DAO) structure. All major decisions are made by the holders of the DAO 1% Club NFTs (only 100 in circulation). Holders of the DAO 1% Club have decision making rights which is managed through an encrypted Telegram server.

    TrumpCoin is currently developing the TrumpCoin Towers XRPL integrated game and is expected to launch early November 2024.

    Key achievements to date are as follows:

    • Tier 1 CEX Listing (Biconomy, ranked top 5 for liquidity with USA/Canadian licenses).
    • Trust Level 1 Status with token image in Xaman.
    • >4,000 holders and >11,000 X followers.
    • AMM farming, faucet rewards and an XRPL telegram trading activity rewards bot.
    • Relationships with 10+ XRP influencers who assist with promotional activity.
    Partnering with HelvetX

    AMM Farming Rewards

    TrumpCoin has implemented a yield/staking mechanism via AMM Farming Rewards in partnership with HelvetX/Tipper. Holders who deposit the $TRUMP/$XRP trading pair into the AMM liquidity pool will receive daily rewards automatically (auto sent via daily payments each night). There is currently >5.5Bn of $TrumpCoin allocated for this purpose. TrumpCoin aims to maintain a yield of 100%+ (across farming rewards and trading fees) and will be reviewed by the DAO committee on a regular basis.

    Central Listings

    CEX Listing and CoinGecko

    As of 7 October 2024, TrumpCoin is listed on Biconomy. Biconomy is considered a tier 1 CEX and boasts a top #5 rank for liquidity, in addition to having Canadian and USA operating licenses. The CEX listing also provides TrumpCoin the foundation to list on CoinGecko, CoinMarketCap and several other listing websites.

    P2E XRPL Game

    XRPL Integrated Game (November 2024)
    TrumpCoin has a team in India who provide assistance with developing new tools (e.g. XRPL activity trading bot). We are partnering with this software agency on a long-term basis to develop an XRPL integrated game called Trump Towers (similar to Zerpmon). The game will be integrated with your wallet and with NFTs. We expect to launch the game in November 2024.

    General

    What is the supply of Trump Coin and is it fixed?

    As Donald J Trump is the 45th President of the United States, the supply of Trump Coin is 45 Billion. During the token issuance stage, the issuing wallet was blackholed. As such no new tokens can be issued and therefore the supply is fixed at 45 Billion (account root).

    Is the TrumpCoin affiliated with the 2024 Donald Trump election campaign?

    Whilst we are huge supporters of Trump, unfortunately the project has no current affiliation with him or his election campaign. In the future we plan to personally reach out to Donald Trump and seek his support in achieving our objective of making crypto great again!

    Why is TrumpCoin built and issued on the XRPL blockchain?

    XRPLs combination of high throughput, low latency, scalability, and developer-friendly features positions it as a competitive option for building decentralized applications and supporting a wide range of use cases.

    What % of the supply was airdropped?

    TrumpCoin completed an initial launch sale and sold 1% of total supply. Subsequently, on 13 June a total of 31.5Bn tokens (70% of supply) was airdropped primarily to holders (68%), with the public also receiving a smaller share (2%).
